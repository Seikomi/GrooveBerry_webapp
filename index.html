GrooveBerry WEB (dev0.1)


Welcome on GrooveBerry WEB interface.
Send one of this commands to remote control the GrooveBerry Server or just click on the corresponding button:

  • #PLAY play the current track of the reading queue
  • #PAUSE pause/unpause the current track of the reading queue
  • #NEXT switch to the next track of the reading queue
  • #PREV switch to the previous track of the reading queue
  • #RANDOMISE randomise the reading queue (new!)
